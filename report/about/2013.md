# First Edition

The first edition of the Open Data Barometer was published in October 2013. 

The report is <a href="http://www.opendataresearch.org/dl/odb2013/Open-Data-Barometer-2013-Global-Report.pdf">available for download here</a>.


## 2013 datasets

The full data used to compute the 2013 Open Data Barometer is archived with the Zenodo research archive at
[https://zenodo.org/collection/user-opendatabarometer](https://zenodo.org/collection/user-opendatabarometer). Zenodo is a
permanent research archive hosted on CERN infrastructure, dedicated to the long-term archival of research data.
